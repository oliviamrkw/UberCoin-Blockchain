How Does UBERCoin Work?

UBERCoins are essentially a cryptocurrency.

UBERCoins can then be contributed to services (eg. towards money off Uber rides), or they can be traded in their own marketplace and exchanged with real currency.

Riding public transit grants the rider a certain number of UBERCoins.

Riders have a unique wallet, similar to a Presto account that credits can be added to.



Earn!

UBERCoins are awarded based on how long your public transit journey was.

Track!

All UBERCoins are stored on the blockchain and are accessible through your wallet id.

Spend!

UBERCoins can be contributed to Uber rides, or can be traded for real money.
